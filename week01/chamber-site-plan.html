Site Name

The Aba Chamber of Commerce

Chosen to reflect the city of Aba, known for its thriving market and business activities. The name highlights the mission of promoting local commerce.

Site Purpose

1. To foster economic growth by promoting local businesses in Aba and providing resources for business development.

2. To connect local entrepreneurs with the resources and networks they need to thrive in the competitive marketplace.

Scenarios

  • What upcoming trade fairs and networking events are available to Aba's business community?
  • How do I become a member of the Aba Chamber of Commerce?
  • What local businesses and services are available in Aba?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Deep Green (#006400)

Used for headings, navigation bar background, and call-to-action buttons.

Secondary Color: Orange (#FF8C00)

Used for links, hover effects, and accent buttons.

Typography

Font 1: Roboto (sans-serif) - Used for body text and paragraphs.

Font 2: Merriweather (serif) - Used for headings and subheadings.

Font 3: Open Sans (sans-serif) (Optional) - Used for secondary headings and navigation.
